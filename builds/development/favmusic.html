Favorite Music

Now even if you don't want to admit, music is and has been an integral part of human lives for a long time. Let's go straight to the point that I am a Metalhead to a great extent. And yeah, just to clarify, my head is not made of metal,it simply means i love Metal genre of music. But I listen to Pop and EDM as well. I generally admire the music and songs which have meaningful lyrics and talk about reality and all the stuff that matters rather than talking about vulgar things.

I started listening to music when I was young but then I stopped for a few years but I resumed in December of 2015 and ever since then I have been a big fan of Linkin Park (American Rock/Metal Band). They are my all time favorites but unfortunately the suicide of the lead vocalist Chester Bennington on 20th July 2017 has left me in a sad and devastated situation.
