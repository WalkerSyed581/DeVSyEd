Favorite Music

Now even if you don't want to admit, music is and has been an integral part of human lives for a long time. Let's go straight to the point that I am a Metalhead to a great extent. And yeah, just to clarify, my head is not made of metal,it simply means i love Metal genre of music. But I listen to Pop and EDM as well. I generally admire the music and songs which have meaningful lyrics and talk about reality and all the stuff that matters rather than talking about vulgar things.

I started listening to music when I was young but then I stopped for a few years but I resumed in December of 2015.

Linkin Park

Ever since I started listening to music I have been a big fan of Linkin Park (American Rock/Metal Band). They are my all time favorites. There music is so diverse ranging from screamy and metal sounding songs to very mellow and calming songs as well. But unfortunately the suicide of the lead vocalist Chester Bennington on 20th July 2017 has left me in a sad and devastated situation. Linkin Park's music has helped millions of people through their hardships.

Metal and Lyrics

Not to get all technical about music because i don't have that much knowledge about it but I want to shed light on what kind of songs I tend to like. I love songs belonging to Metal genre but that is not the only genre I like. As far as songs are concerned I only like those songs which are lyrically good or even mediocre otherwise I run away fast. For me metal is something that pumps me up. That screaming and headbanging as well as the music is a great motivation for me. Sometimes well i am feeling very bad about something i listen to a screamy metal song and it goes away. I also like songs and bands that have astronomical jargon in their lyrics and that spacy feeling in the music like Starset.
