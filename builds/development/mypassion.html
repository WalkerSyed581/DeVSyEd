Intro

Hi There! Visitor of My Site!The site you have landed upon belongs to Syed Sajeel Hassan.

It is kinda like a mostly personal and a portfolio site mashed together to form what you see before your eyes.I am from the Northern Province of Pakistan and I am (or soon to become) a Front-End Web Developer. On this website you will learn about me as a Web Developer and a fellow human. Because that’s what this site is all about as you may have guessed from the title. I talk about my skills in web development and about personal things as well. You can also Hire Me for your Web Project.

Where i Learned

I learned Web Development as a professional mostly from Lynda.com. It is one of the most great resource for almost any topic. As far as Web Design and Development are concerned, all the courses are pretty good and go very in-depth into the topic. The Exercise Files available for each course are very helpful but you can also practice on the same files online on the site without even downloading them.

My Passion

Passion!!Man that is such a heavy yet beautiful word. Enlighten me if you know why something becomes your passion but mine is to do Web Development. I love doing it. I won’t say ''more than anything'' because there are some other things as well *wink* *wink*. Web Development is the reason why I am currently making this site so as to pursue this field as a career.

This is the field for which I, after finishing my 2nd year in college, am not taking admission in a university so that I can chase my dream job and hopefully I soon will be successful if God wills. I am a Freelance Web Developer and I love it. One of the main reason I love it is because of the freedom I have in this field and importantly I can make my imagination go wild in this field along with other more important reasons as well.

Web Development

First Experience

Web Development is a field I was introduced to by my Uncle when I was 16 or so. I was told by my cousin that this field can get me a long way in life and I was intrigued. Once I started learning HTML and CSS, I was like Woah!! This is the most fun and best thing I have ever done. It was like a new world was opened in front of me. I wanted to learn more of it, things like JavaScript, but could not at that time because of school and I had to go to college the next year so I stopped.

Professional Study

I resumed learning web development on 12th May 2017 and have loved the few months journey up to this point. Web Development to a large and Programming to a small extent are the sort of things to me which make you feel like you have actually done something instead of memorizing what people have already made and writing that on paper.

Why I like It?

Now it could have been the time of me telling you stories behind me liking this field which I so can't right now, so I will leave you with this list of reasons:

  1. The control and freedom it provides to you for making anything you want and have the power to spread it to the whole world is just amazing.
  2. The learning process is very enjoyable and the joy you feel when you practice a new language or a feature and you get it right is beyond the constraint of words.
  3. The great thing about this field is that the experts and pros within it are willing to share their experience with the newbies so that anyone can learn it with an internet connection.
  4. Most importantly for me, the uncertainty of the work makes it even more amazing and motivates you to work hard while plunging into its uncertain depths is an amazing experience.
  5. And of course being called a person who makes the World Wide Web, which everyone uses day in and day out, is also a pretty good feeling.
